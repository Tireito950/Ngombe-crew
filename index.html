ABOUT US

Developing Responsible Leaders Through Service and Adventure

Ngombe Rover Crew is a Rover Scout unit under the Kenya Scouts Association dedicated to empowering young adults through leadership, service, outdoor adventure, teamwork, environmental conservation, and lifelong learning.

Our members participate in community service projects, leadership training, camping expeditions, environmental conservation, first aid training, mentorship programmes, and national scouting events.

WHY JOIN US

Why Join Ngombe Rover Crew?

Discover opportunities that shape character, develop leadership, and create lifelong friendships.

Leadership Development

Develop confidence, communication, decision-making, and project management skills through practical leadership opportunities.

Environmental Conservation

Participate in tree planting, river clean-ups, climate action, and environmental education initiatives.

Community Service

Serve schools, hospitals, children's homes, and local communities through volunteer programmes.

Adventure

Camping, mountain hiking, backpacking, survival training, navigation, and outdoor expeditions.

Life Skills

First aid, public speaking, financial literacy, career development, problem-solving, and teamwork.

International Opportunities

Participate in national camps, international jamborees, exchange programmes, and global scouting events.
