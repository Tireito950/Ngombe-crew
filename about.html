ABOUT US

Welcome to Ng'ombe Rover Crew

Ng'ombe Rover Crew is a vibrant community of young adults united by the principles of Scouting, leadership, service, and adventure. As part of the Kenya Scouts Association, we are committed to empowering our members with practical life skills, strong character, and a spirit of service that positively transforms our communities.

Our Crew brings together passionate individuals who believe that leadership is earned through action, teamwork, and dedication. Whether we are organizing community outreach programs, participating in environmental conservation, hiking through Kenya's beautiful landscapes, or mentoring young Scouts, we strive to leave every place better than we found it.

🎯

Our Mission

To develop responsible, skilled, and compassionate leaders through community service, outdoor adventure, lifelong learning, and the values of Scouting.

🔭

Our Vision

To become a leading Rover Scout Crew recognized for excellence in leadership, innovation, environmental stewardship, and community impact throughout Kenya.

🤝

Our Promise

"To empower young people through service, adventure, leadership, and lifelong learning while making a positive difference in our communities."
